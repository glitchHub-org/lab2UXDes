Activity 2:
For the 5 favorite application that I use daily basis are: Steam, Google Drive and One Drive, Honey, GeForce Experience and Rainbow Six Seige. Steam is a video game distributor application that is a consumer-friendly game distributor and supports the independent game studios that just started. Steam has an easy-to-browse game library UI that highlights the game that you have downloaded on your PC and the ones you haven’t downloaded in your PC. My second favorite application that I use is Google Drive and One drive, this application is a cloud storage app that I use since I don’t carry a flash drive on me and I use it to save my work files online so I can just see the things I have on that cloud storage. My 3rd favorite application will be Honey, Honey is a browser extension on Google Chrome that I use to get a discount on the things I buy online, this finds you discount codes online on whatever things you’ve bought online. My 4th favorite application will be my GeForce experience, this application optimizes your game graphics that your system can handle and I use the it’s feature called Shadow Play that I use to record my gameplay on my games that I play that I want or a situation that’s way too funny to not record. Lastly will be my favorite game, Rainbow Six Siege, this game is available for PC, Xbox one and PS4, this game is a competitive game that I really enjoy since I like to trash other people online or fool around with my friends since we barely take the game seriously, what I like the most on this game is not its graphics but its gameplay, the gameplay is not hyper realistic like other FPS games like Arma 3 but it’s realistic enough for having breakable surfaces and an one-shot headshot policy that I barely land since I have bad accuracy.
